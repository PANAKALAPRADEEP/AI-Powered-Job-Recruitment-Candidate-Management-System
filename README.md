# AI-Powered Job Recruitment & Candidate Management System

## 📌 Project Overview

The **AI-Powered Job Recruitment & Candidate Management System** is developed on the **Salesforce Platform** to automate and optimize the recruitment process. The application enables recruiters and hiring managers to manage job openings, candidate applications, AI-driven evaluations, approval workflows, and hiring decisions from a centralized platform.

## ⚙️ Project Workflow

✅ Recruiter logs into Salesforce.

            ⬇️

✅ Create a Job Opening.

            ⬇️

✅ Candidate submits an application.

            ⬇️

✅ AI evaluates the candidate profile.

            ⬇️

✅ Candidate receives an AI score.

            ⬇️

✅ Interview is scheduled.

            ⬇️

✅ Offer is generated.

            ⬇️

✅ High salary offers go through the Approval Process.

            ⬇️

✅ Hiring Manager approves or rejects the offer.

            ⬇️

✅ Candidate hiring is completed.
